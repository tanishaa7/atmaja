Welcome to Atmaja

Atmaja is a voluntary organization where all members are adoptive families. We are a vibrant community based in Kolkata, dedicated to supporting adoptive parents through every step of their journey.

‡§Ü‡§§‡•ç‡§Æ‡§ú‡§æ - "Born of the Soul"

We believe that the womb is not central to parent-child relationships. Love, care, and commitment create the true bonds of family.

Atmaja works towards creating awareness about adoption, advocating for adoptive parents' rights, and building a supportive network for families.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support to adoptive families through information sharing, emotional guidance, legal assistance, and community building. We advocate for policy changes and work towards making adoption a celebrated path to parenthood.
